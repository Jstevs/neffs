Neffs Canyon

Neffs Canyon small spur canyon that runs from the base of mount Olympus. The trail is primarily an old jeep track but has splinter trails all the way going up. Also there is no signs requiring dogs to be on leash at this time. The end of the hike offers a view that makes it worth the trip.
This is a pretty easy hike that is good for all skill levels. Although it is uphill.

Trail Details
Distance: 2.75 miles
Time: 2 hours
Difficulty: Easy
