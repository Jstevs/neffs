Neffs Canyon

Neffs Canyon is a small spur canyon that runs from the base of Mount Olympus. Dogs are allowed as long as you stay in Neff's Canyon and don't go on Mount Olympus. Dogs are allowed off leash. The trail is primarily an old jeep track but has smaller trails all the way going up. The views are beautiful no matter which trail you choose.
This hike is mostly uphill. Some areas are fairly steep, but not technical.

Trail Details
Distance: 2.75 miles
Time: 2 hours
Difficulty: Easy
